Tips that we learnt from our Travels

General Tips

  • Pack essentials like clothes, toiletries, and gadgets.
  • Keep both digital and physical copies of travel documents (passport, visa, etc.).
  • Bring basic medications and check vaccination requirements.

Cultural and Language Awareness

  • Learn basic phrases in the local language.
  • Research cultural etiquette to avoid misunderstandings.

Budget and Transportation

  • Exchange currency at trusted locations and set a daily budget.
  • Research local transportation options, like prepaid transit cards or ride-hailing apps.

Accommodation and Sustainability

  • Choose accommodations based on location, budget, and reviews.
  • Use reusable water bottles and support local businesses.
